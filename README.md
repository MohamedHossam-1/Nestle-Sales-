# 🍫 Nestle Sales Performance Dashboard (2013-2014)

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Nestle's sales data over a two-year period. It tracks key performance indicators (KPIs), regional distribution, product performance, and sales representative efficiency to drive data-driven commercial decisions.

## 🛠️ Key Features & Visuals
* **Executive Summary KPIs:** Real-time tracking of **Total Sales (10M)** and **Average Price (301.28)**.
* **Temporal Trends:** A line chart visualizing price fluctuations by year and quarter, highlighting significant market changes between 2013 and 2014.
* **Product Analysis:** A bar chart identifying top-performing products, led by **Nestle Smarties Pop-Up (1.50M)**.
* **Geographical Insights:** * **Regional Pie Chart:** Breakdown of sales across North (35.75%), West, South, and East regions.
    * **Map Visualization:** Geographic mapping of sales distribution across various cities.
* **Workforce Performance:** A detailed ranking of Sales Representatives by their total sales contribution.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Filtering capabilities by **City** (Agra, Ahmedabad, Bangalore, etc.) for granular analysis.

## 📊 Key Insights
1. **Regional Leader:** The **North Region** is the primary revenue driver, accounting for over **35%** of total sales.
2. **Product Success:** Smarties Pop-Up and Nestle Fab are the flagship products, contributing significantly to the 1.5M+ sales bracket.
3. **Sales Spikes:** The trend analysis shows a massive surge in the sum of price/sales during late 2013 and late 2014, indicating strong Q4 seasonal performance.
4. **Pricing Consistency:** With an average price of 301.28, the dashboard helps monitor price stability across different regions.

## 🚀 How to Explore
1. Download the `.pbix` file from this repository.
2. Open it using **Power BI Desktop**.
3. Interact with the **City Slicers** and **Visuals** to filter data and uncover specific regional trends.
